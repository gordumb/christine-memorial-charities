Christine McCreary
Memorial Charities

Christine is a sister, daughter, grandchild, niece, cousin, and friend who passed away too soon. In her will she left the following list of eleven charities she wanted to support, and in her memory we would like to gently extend the opportunity for others to support them in her honor.

  • World Wildlife Fund

    The World Wildlife Fund is a global charity that seeks to "build a future in which people live in harmony with nature" by working to protect Earth's biodiversity through the use of sustainable practices to reduce humanity's impact on the planet.

    Donate to World Wildlife Fund

  • Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center

    The Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center is driven by the compassionate initiative to advance the care and prevention of cancer with scientific innovations.

    Donate to Fred Hutch Center

  • The Humane Society for Tacoma & Pierce County

    The Humane Society partners with the Tacoma and Pierce County communities to provide life-saving work for pets in need and support to the people who love them.

    Donate to the Humane Society

  • Gary Sinise Foundation

    The Gary Sinise Foundation serves America by honoring the defenders, veterans, first responders, their families, and those in need by creating and supporting unique programs designed to entertain, educate, inspire, strengthen, and build communities.

    Donate to the Gary Sinise Foundation

  • Puyallup Animal Rescue

    The Puyallup Animal Rescue is a local non-profit rescue group dedicated to finding permanent homes for homeless dogs and cats.

    Donate to Puyallup Animal Rescue

  • Conservation Northwest

    Conservation Northwest protects, connects and restores wildlands and wildlife from the Washington Coast to the British Columbia Rockies.

    Donate to Conservation Northwest

  • Ovarian Cancer Research Alliance

    OCRA is dedicated to curing ovarian cancer while improving treatments by advancing innovative science, promoting preventive measures, and advocating for, educating and supporting anyone affected by gynecologic cancers to ensure the best possible care and outcomes.

    Donate to OCRA

  • Project HEAL

    Project HEAL aims to break down systemic, healthcare, and financial barriers to eating disorder healing.

    Donate to Project HEAL

  • Mental Health America

    Mental Health America advances the mental health and well-being of all people living in the U.S. through public education, research, advocacy and public policy, and direct service.

    Donate to Mental Health America

  • National Alliance for Eating Disorders

    The National Alliance for Eating disorders is a national non-profit organization providing education, referrals, and support for all individuals experiencing eating disorders, as well as their loved ones.

    Donate to The Alliance

  • Emerald Ridge High School Scholarship

    The Emerald Ridge High School staff scholarship is awarded to a student that demonstrates leadership and maintains a 2.8 GPA. The recipient will be determined by the ERHS Staff Leadership Team.

    Donate to ERHS Scholarship
